Help us to build AI models that know about your culture!

We're a team of international researchers working together to build AI models that truly understand and represent cultures from around the world, and we need your help!
We're looking for images and short captions that capture typical and meaningful aspects of your culture: food, traditions, clothing, celebrations, and more. Your contributions will help make future AI technologies more inclusive and culturally aware.
As a thank-you, we'll recognize top contributors with rewards at the end of the campaign.

Concepts

We have identified five key categories for collecting images and descriptions of culturally relevant concepts. Our local language leads from different countries have already gathered concepts that represent their cultural setting. You're welcome to explore and contribute to the concepts they've provided, or feel free to add entirely new ones that capture other parts of your culture.

Food and Beverages

Traditional dishes, local ingredients, and beverages that are common in your region. This includes everyday meals, special occasion foods, and drinks that are part of your cultural practices.

Clothing

Traditional garments, everyday clothing styles, and accessories that are typical in your culture. This covers both formal attire for ceremonies and casual wear that reflects local fashion or customs.

Buildings (interior and exterior)

Architectural styles, home layouts, and building designs that are characteristic of your area. This includes both traditional structures and contemporary buildings that reflect local building practices.

Instruments, Tools and Utensils

Objects used in daily life, work, cooking, and cultural practices. This covers traditional tools, kitchen utensils, musical instruments, and equipment specific to local crafts or professions.

Celebrations, Sports and Events

Cultural festivals, religious ceremonies, sports activities, and community gatherings that are common or important in your culture. This includes both large public events and smaller more private practices.

Why is it important to understand the representation of culture in AI?

Current AI systems often reflect the cultural biases of their training data, which predominantly comes from Western, English-speaking sources. This creates significant gaps in how AI models understand and represent the world's diverse cultures.
Research has shown that popular AI models struggle to accurately represent non-Western cultures. For example, when prompted to generate images of "a wedding," these systems typically produce Western-style ceremonies with white dresses and church settings, failing to capture the rich diversity of wedding traditions worldwide. Similarly, food-related prompts often default to Western cuisine, even when the context suggests otherwise.
These biases have practical consequences. AI-powered search engines may not surface culturally relevant results for non-Western users. Virtual assistants may not understand cultural references or practices outside their training scope. Educational AI tools may inadvertently promote cultural stereotypes or omit important cultural knowledge.
By collecting culturally diverse data with local community input, we aim to help researchers build more inclusive AI systems that better represent global cultural diversity.
